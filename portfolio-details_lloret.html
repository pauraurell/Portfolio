I made this experience from the blocking until the final delivery of the project. I made 3D assets and integrated them in Unity for the four scenes of the experience. Also made all animations of the environments and constantly checked the progress with the client in a iterative process.

This was the first experience I made all by my own from beginnig to end and I learnt a lot of about Unity timelines, and scene optimization. The idea behind this experience was to warn visitors about climate change with a journey starting on a beautiful and filled with life submarine environment, then going through a forest to finally land on a polluted dystopic world full of garbage.
