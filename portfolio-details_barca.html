Barça Virtual Dream

Project information

  • Category: Univrse VR Immersive Experience
  • Project date: June, 2023
  • Location: FC Barcelona Stadium
  • Project URL:
    FCB, Virtual Dream

As a huge FC Barcelona fan this project is one of my favourite works. This project has seven scenes across a FC Barcelona metaverse, I worked on two of them, creating/integrating assets, doing the lighting, creating the avatars and optimizing the scenes. One was the Locker Room where you could inteact with the lockers and see videos of Barça legendary players highlights inside of them. The other one was the Avatar Selector Room, where you could pick your favourite iconic player to become him/her.
